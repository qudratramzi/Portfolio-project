Optimization of the WWTP

This project focuses on improving the performances of the San Giovanni di Grosseto Wastewater Treatment Plant by analyzing its current processes and identifying where efficiency can be increased. I evaluated the existing treatment line, simulated plant behavior using the ASIM2d activated sludge model, and proposed upgrades such as an additional biological line, improved secondary clarification, and enhanced sludge management. The goal was to reduce operational issues, improve effluent quality, and make the plant more resilient to future loads and environmental requirements.
